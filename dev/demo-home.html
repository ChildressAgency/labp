Where to Stay

High Point Marina

High Point Marina is a full-service, on-stop-shop for all your fishing and boating needs while you are visiting beautiful Lake Anna, Va. The Lighthouse Inn offers excellent accommodations and is the only waterfront motel on the lake. The rooms are clean and well furnished.

The dock is less than 70' from your room!

Map & Directions

Lake Anna State Park

Covering more tha 13,000 acres with over 250 miles of shore line located in Louisa, orange and Spotsylvania counties, Lake Anna is one of the largest freshwater inland lakes in Virginia.

Close to Richmond, Fredericksburg and Charlottesville, Lake Anna is 72 miles south of Washington D.C. and easy to reach from anywhere in Virginia.
