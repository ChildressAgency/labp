About the LABP

The Lake Anna Business Partnership envisions a business environment that stimulates responsible commercial development providing goods and services basic to a safe and secure, comfortable and convenient, community in partnership with like-minded citizens, local governments and civic organizations that seek to preserve for future generations the treasure of Lake Anna.

Our Mission

The Lake Anna Business Partnership exists to promote a strong and growing business community serving the lake region, and to work with organizations throughout and beyond Virginia to increase interest in the lake as a tourism, investment and residence destination. At the same time, LABP works to preserve the beauty and safety of the Lake Anna region for current residents and future generations.

Who we are

The Lake Anna Business Partnership, composed of businesses and professional organizations and people serving the Lake Anna area, is a non-profit organization created in 2001 to promote business and responsible growth that preserves the intrinsic beauty of the Lake Anna area. Our members are involved community leaders and dedicated business professionals committed to each others' success within a robust and responsible business community.

What we do

The partnership works through member volunteers, an elected board of directors and functional committees to support responsible growth around the lake through communication, government liaison, events and cooperation with other organizations supporting the lake. The LSBP also sponsors a series of performance and social events providing members an opportunity to network, and to raise funds for the organizations initiatives.

Our community

Whether looking to find the right business solution or to be the right business resource, your answer can be found within the Lake Anna Business Partnership. The LABP is the utmost successful business networking group at Lake Anna, where the premier community businesses work cohesively to bring better business practices to Lake Anna. The Lake Anna Business Partnership is your solution to finding the best business resources in the area.
